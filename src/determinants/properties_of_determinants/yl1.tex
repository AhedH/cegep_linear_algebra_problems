Let $A$ and $B$ be $\nbyn{n}$ matrices such that $AB=-BA$ and $n$ is odd, show that either $A$ or $B$ has no inverse.
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
Hint: Apply the determinant to both sides $AB=-BA$.
\end{Answer}
